## Quick Tutorial

Kick off a new project in Kayel Calleja:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Dashboard">
    Go to `https://app.kayelcalleja.com` and log in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Project">
    Click the "New Project" button in the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Details">
    Enter your project name and description, then click "Create".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Documenting">
    Add your first page or invite team members from the project dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
