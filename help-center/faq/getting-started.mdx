## Quick Start Guide

You can start using Kayel Calleja in minutes:

1. Visit `https://app.kayelcalleja.com`
2. Click "Sign Up" and fill in your details
3. Confirm your email
4. Log in to your dashboard

## First Steps

- Create a new project from the dashboard
- Add team members under "Collaborators"
- Start documenting your project instantly

<Callout kind="info">
Kayel Calleja is free to try with unlimited public projects.
</Callout>
