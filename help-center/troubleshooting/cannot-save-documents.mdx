## Symptoms

- Edits not saving
- "Save failed" error message

## Causes

- Browser offline
- Session expired

## Quick Fix

    ````bash
    ping app.kayelcalleja.com
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Re-login">
    Log out and sign in again to refresh your session.
  </Step>
</Steps>
